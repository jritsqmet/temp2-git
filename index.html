CLASSIC TERROR

Relive the golden age of survival horror — where fear had no saves, corridors whispered secrets, and every shadow was alive.

SILENT HILL
1999 · PS1
Fog-drenched psychological horror where a father searches for his adopted daughter in a nightmarish town consumed by cults and guilt.
RESIDENT EVIL
1996 · PS1
The survival-horror pioneer. Explore Spencer Mansion, manage scarce ammo, and uncover the Umbrella conspiracy. Classic fixed-camera dread.
ALONE IN THE DARK
1992 · PC
The true grandfather of 3D survival horror. Investigate Derceto Manor, battle Lovecraftian creatures, and solve eerie puzzles.
FATAL FRAME
2001 · PS2
Defeat vengeful ghosts with the Camera Obscura. Japanese horror at its most tragic and terrifying. Atmosphere beyond compare.
CLOCK TOWER
1995 · SNES
Point-and-click terror where a giant scissor-wielding man stalks you. Multiple endings and pure panic hide-and-seek mechanics.
RULE OF ROSE
2006 · PS2
Disturbing psychological fairy tale. Aboard an airship, a young woman faces a cruel aristocracy of children. Deeply unsettling.
